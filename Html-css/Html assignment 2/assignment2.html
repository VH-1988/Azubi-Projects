Naming VH

Telling my story

It was the year 1988 in a small remote village called Lwandlane Location,
 where a small energetic baby boy full of smiles was born.
The whole village gathered together to celebrate the birth of new life,
and as the men and women of this small hard-working village
in the mountains of Eastern Cape sat around the fire,
they thought to themselves what are we going to call this boy,
Ngonyama(lion),
said one villager for he is going to be strong and fierce like a lion.

Other names came up but there was something about this baby,
as his smile brought joy and warmed the hearts of the villagers,
with much thought on what to name him the perfect name was given to him
and he carried its meaning to this day.
The name he was given is Vuyolwethu(our joy).
